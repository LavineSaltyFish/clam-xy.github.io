Abstract

The purpose of this final project is: finding the relationship among philosophers and learning about philosophers' thoughts. This final project contains two parts: drawing networks and their structure analysis, NLTK processing and analysis of books. attribute analysis, degree analysis, nltk analysis; The network has 10018 nodes and links.
