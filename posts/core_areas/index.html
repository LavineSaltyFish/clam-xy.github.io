Core areas

2021-12-01
5 min read
desciption page

In this section we look at several important areas of philosophy:

  • Epistemologists: the critical study of science and scientific knowledge and the study of knowledge in general.

  • Metaphysicians: the fundamental questions of first principles of being, nothingness, identity and change

  • Logicians: the science of reasoning and relationships between ideas

  • Politicaltheorists : the philosophical study of government

  • Aestheticians : deals with questions of beauty and artistic taste

  • Indexsociopoliticalthinkers : focuses to social issues

  • Political : all fields related to politics (broader than the previous two)

  • Ethicists : ethics and ethical codes

  • Firstly, we find that there are many more philosophers who are linked to the field of politics. The fact that politics touches on so many different issues probably explains this.

The most linked eras :

There are two characteristic groups here, one focused on the political and social domain and another focused on metaphysics.

  • In the first group there is a strong link between politics and ethics. Ethics studies human behaviour and is very closely linked to the laws and human rights that the government implements. More surprisingly, we find aesthetics, probably because the latter domain is in opposition to the rigidity of a state and its rules.

  • The second group focuses on areas that are more theoretical in contrast to politics, aesthetics and ethics which are very applied. There is thus a strong link between metaphysics, logic and epistemology.

It is interesting to note that the fields of politics are less connected to logic and scientific reasoning but rather to beauty and human ethics.

These bag of words correspond to the scientific and theoretical fields of philosophy.

  • We notice that logic focuses on less applied issues. We find very famous scientists and mathematicians (Turring, Hilbert) and we find the words algebra and mathematical.

  • For epistemology the words and personalities are more related to the scientific study of the body and well-being. We find Piegat who was a recognized pediatrician or the word antroposophy which is the study of the body. We also see the theme of scientific doubt with the words development, preoperational and pyrronhist which is philosophical doubt.

  • Metaphysics is much more related to spiritual research. Indeed we find Malebranche, Teilhard or Eckhard who were important religious men. It is therefore interesting to note that the search for knowledge of the being in philosophy has been greatly influenced by religion. The being, in order to conceive itself, very often places itself in relation to a guide, a superior being that can be found in religion. This brings to light the study of the links between the philosophical domains. The separation between the political and metaphysical domains can also be seen as an opposition between state and religion.
