Connections among attributes

2021-12-01
5 min read
desciption page

This page shows the analysis of connections among fields, theories and eras. Philosophy has many fields and has born many theories, if one field connects strongly with one or some specific theories? In other words, if one philosopher belong to one field prefers to research one or specific theories? In addition, did philosophy change a lot with time going on? If some theories or fields just created or being popular in recent years? One weight network is built to analyse the connections among these three attributes. The following is the result and how I found it😎.

Extracting elements and build dataset

To build one network, corresponding dataset need to create first💡. Therefore, all the philosophers' name extracted in “Lists of philosophers by era”, then finding their research field and research theories in “Lists of philosophers by field” and “Lists of philosophers by philosophical theory” in wikipedia by using BeautifulSoup. Finally, one excel with philosophers and their research fields, theories and eras is saved by using DataFrame.

Wikipedia does not have one complete list, because phiosophers in various list is different, which really troubles me when collecting data.🙉

Building weight network

One network is combined with nodes and edges, for weight network, it also has weight in each edge. In this network:

  • Nodes are different classes of eras(18th, 19th, 20th), theories(atheist, humanist, utilitarian, rationalist, environmental) and fields(science, mind, religion, feminist, language).

  • For edges, the classes of different attributes of one philosopher links each other, namely if one philosopher born in twenty era prefers environmental theory and belong to science field. Then these three nodes will be connected with each other which creates three links.

  • The weight is the number of specific link.

Below is the weight network created by using networkx:

Figure 1:This graph shows the weight network, width of edge correspond to it’s weight. Sky blue nodes belong to “era”, dark blue nodes belong to “field” and dark yellow nodes belong to “theory”.

Degree distribution

Degree of a node in a network is the number of connections it has to other nodes and the degree distribution is the probability distribution of these degrees over the whole network. Below is the degree distribution in this network drawed by using matplotlib:

Figure 2:The first five nodes belong to “field” attribute, the middle five nodes belong to “theory” attribute and the last three nodes are eras.

According to this degree distribution. Degree of 19 and 18 era is less than 10 whcih means some theories or field do not build link to them, therefore there are some new theories or fields do not created or not common before 20th. Degree of “science”, “mind”, “atheist” and “utilitarian” is 8 which means they have links with all eras and theories or fields, therefore these fields or theories are rich in content and live long time. Meanwhile, degree of environmental is lowest with 4, so environmental theory is very young, maybe we need to appeal more people to focus on our environment.🌏

Weight heatmap

Weight is the number of one edge and heatmap is data visualization technique that shows magnitude of a phenomenon as color in two dimensions.

To draw heatmap, DataFrame need first, it uses nodes name as colunmns and indexs, value is the weight between each, it shows below:

Figure 3:The table takes nodes as columns and indexs, the value is corresponds to the weight of them.

Then, the heatmap is drawn by using seaborn:

Figure 4:The heatmap shows the weight between nodes.

As shown in figure4, weight for link between “twenty” and “athesit” is the biggest as 30 and that of link between “twenty” and “utilitarian”, “religion”, “religion” and “feminist” is low with value around 2 to 4. It seems much more people believe science now 🚀. “Athesit” connect strongly with “science”, “mind” and “language” field. “Environmental” and “rationalist” have less weight with other nodes. “Utilitarian” has bigger weight links with “science” and “mind” while the link to “feminist”, “language” and “religion”. “Humanists” has strong link with “science” while the weight of links with other theories is small.

Conclusion

That’s all the content of my analysis in this part, now it is time for conclusion.👏

According to analysis above, the number of philosophers is growing rapidly in 20th! Maybe our life is much more better than before which makes people have time and energy to study philosophy. ✌️

For eras, some theories and fields is becoming popular, such as: mind, science, language in field and environmental, atheist, rationalist and humanists. Especially, for atheist, rationlist, science, mind, environmental and feminist, the weight increases quickly, which means philosophers are focuing on law of nature instead of superstition and attach importance to humen being and humen equality.

For fields and theories, mind, science and language have more links with other theories, which are much more popular than feminist and religion. And science connects with atheist strongly, which makes sense. Mind and language are both connect with atheist strongly, which means philosophers focus on the evolution of human being instead of believing God creates humanity and language. And for environmental and rationalist theories, they are emerging theories which only appears in 20th. This shows the influence of science to philosophy, more people focus on material world and the relationship between environment and human being.

To sum up, the number of philosophers is growing, especially for philosophers in “science” and “atheist”, more types of fields and theories are creating as well. And philosophers focus on humanity and real world under the significant influence of modern science and technology.
